About me

Aspiring Artpreneur

Hello there and thank you for opening my Art-Portfolio. My name is Sammy Nurrein, a passionate artist specializing in Traditional Art. My work mainly explores Anime Themed Art, inspired by my love for Anime and cartoons. My goal is to create Art that people feel amazing to look at and also resonate with. I draw from Inspos/references that is, either Anime I admire, real life photos of people, Archictecture, nature and Imagination. I believe that Art is subjective and resonates with people differently depending on ones position in life. In my Journey, I am still trying to learn new skills of Art everyday because Art is very vast. I've had the opportunity to showcase my Art on Social Media pages like Instagram and Tiktok. I have also shared my Art, in my Art-club where we do weekly submissions on Friday. I am yet to work with Art-galleries and other mediums to market my Art and make a living out of it. Art is a very great way of connecting with people. So whether you have arrived at my Portfolio to explore, discuss collaborations, work together,commissions, buy an Art piece, or even if you want me to make you a custom piece. I invite you to dive into my world. Feel free to reach out to me on Email or Phone Number below. I would love to connect with you, fellow Artist, Creators and Dreamers.
